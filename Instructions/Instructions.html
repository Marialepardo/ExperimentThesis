The experiments - in short:

  • This study consists of two parts.
  • In the first part you will be making binary purchasing decisions. In total there will be 38 decisions.
  • In the second part you will complete a short questionnare. Everything you fill in will be anonymous.
  • The products that you will observe are
    real

Purchasing Decisions

  • At the beginning of the experiment you will be endowed with 10 euros.
  • Of all the participants who finish the survey, 15% will be randomly selected to be paid out for their decisions.
  • In every round you will be making purchasing decisions and you will be able to use your endowment to pay for them.
  • Although you will be making 48 decisions, only one will be randomly selected and that item will be purchased and given to you.
  • Therefore, the optimal strategy is for every round to choose the product you would like to purchase.
  • If you would not buy any of the products for yourself, please imagine that you are buying the product on behalf of someone else.
  • We ask you to make the following decisions as if you were in a real supermarket. Therefore, always choose the product that you would actually pay for.
